About Me

Hello! My name is Andrew Yu and I am a current student in the UW bootcamp course. Prior to this program I was a Bartender for the Din Tai Fung Restaurant company and a small time Twitch streamer (Link below). I have been in customer service for nearly 10 years now. And after so much time in this ive decided to make a career change with this program.

It took some time and a bit of research for me to commit to this program but after this first week I must say that the curriculum is very well made and give us students so many tools to succeed! BEST OF LUCK TO ALL AND THROUGH COLLABORATION WE SHALL ACHIEVE GREATNESS!!

In my spare time I enjoy traveling to explore new places! Mostly its for food but overall all my travels have been great adventures! I also power lift with my inspiration coming from a group called Barbell Brigade. They were originally a YouTube group of individuals that ended opening their own fitness gym in Los Angles. Another thing I enjoy is surfing the website specifically on YouTube to find creative inspiration. A big influence is a group of Taiwanese Americans called Wong-Fu Productions. Their content for me is always relatable and ive been a fan since they first began! (all Links are below)
